Recent Getaway

This semester, I studied abroad in London. I was very fortunate to travel to a lot of places. My favorite place I traveled to was Barcelona. I swam in the mediterranean, ate tapas, and took a four hour bike tour around the city. It was so beautiful and I really hope to go back!

Restaurant

My favorite restaurant is Sticks N' Sushi . The owner of the restaurant's mom is Japanese and their dad is Danish. I love seafood and the fish is always fresh. I usually get the Pink Alaska roll, Seaweed Salad, and a Miso Cod stick. They have locations in London and Copenhagen. My favorite location is in Copenhagen because it is on the roof and has a beautiful view of the city. The restaurant is a bit expensive but it is perfect for a special occasion!

Favorite Food

My favorite food is fresh tomatoes. I like to eat them with salt, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar. I also enjoy cooked tomatoes, tomato soup, and fresh tomatoes on pizza and pasta. I will eat a tomato in any form!


Favorite Books

Name Cover Image Author Summary
Educated Tara Westover "Educated" by Tara Westover is a memoir recounting her journey from growing up in a strict, survivalist family in rural Idaho with no formal education to earning a PhD from Cambridge University, highlighting the transformative power of education. The book explores themes of family loyalty, self-invention, and the pursuit of knowledge.
Things We Never Got Over Lucy Score "Things We Never Got Over" by Lucy Score is a contemporary romance novel about Naomi, who escapes to the small town of Knockemout after her life is upended by her estranged twin sister, only to find herself entangled with a grumpy but irresistible barber named Knox. As Naomi tries to rebuild her life and care for her niece, she discovers love and a sense of belonging in unexpected places.
Fancy Nancy Jane O'Connor In "Fancy Nancy" by Jane O'Connor, young Nancy loves everything fancy, from her clothes to her vocabulary, and tries to elevate her family's lifestyle with her sophisticated flair. Despite her efforts, she learns that being fancy is less important than being together with her loved ones.
Little House on the Prairie Laura Ingalls Wilder "Little House on the Prairie" by Laura Ingalls Wilder recounts the adventures of the Ingalls family as they move to the Kansas prairie and build a new life in a frontier settlement. The story highlights their resilience, encounters with Native Americans, and the challenges of living in an untamed land.
